orbit simulator instructions

use arrow keys or W/A/S/D to control your spaceship:
  • W / ↑ → thrust forward
  • S / ↓ → brake / slow down
  • A / ← → rotate left
  • D / → → rotate right
gravity from planets pulls you toward them - avoid collisions!
trails show your path through space.
try to navigate through multiple planets without hitting them.
checkpoints are automatically set after passing planets; if you collide, you'll respawn at the last checkpoint.
you have 3 lives.
