Orbit Simulator

Navigate your spaceship through gravity fields of planets. Avoid collisions!

Controls:
W / ↑ : Thrust forward
S / ↓ : Brake / slow down
A / ← : Rotate left
D / → : Rotate right

Trails show your path through space.
Checkpoints are automatically set after passing planets; if you collide, you'll respawn at the last checkpoint.
You have 3 lives.
